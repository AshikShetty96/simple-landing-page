Welcome To Udupi & Mangalore

Udupi

Temples:

  • Sri Krishna Math - One of the most famous temples in Udupi.
  • Udupi Sri Krishna Temple - A historic temple dedicated to Lord Krishna.
  • Anegudde Vinayaka Temple - Known for its beautiful architecture and religious significance.

Don't forget to attend the mesmerizing evening aarti at Sri Krishna Math!

Beaches:

  • Kapu Beach - Famous for its golden sands and clear waters.
  • St. Mary's Island - A unique geological formation with stunning rock formations.

Enjoy a day of sunbathing and beach volleyball at Kapu Beach!

Nature and Parks:

  • Malpe Beach - A picturesque beach offering various water sports activities.
  • River Swarna - Enjoy a serene boat ride along the beautiful River Swarna.

Take a peaceful stroll along the banks of River Swarna and enjoy the lush greenery!

Historical Sites:

  • Shri Indrani Park - Explore the ruins of an ancient fort and enjoy panoramic views of the city.
  • Bahubali Statue - Admire the towering statue of Bahubali, a symbol of peace and spirituality.

Climb up to Shri Indrani Park for breathtaking views of Udupi!

Mangalore

Temples:

  • Kadri Manjunath Temple - One of the oldest temples in Mangalore.
  • Mangaladevi Temple - Dedicated to Goddess Mangaladevi, the presiding deity of Mangalore.
  • St. Aloysius Chapel - Known for its beautiful frescoes and architecture.

Experience the spiritual ambiance at these ancient temples!

Beaches:

  • Panambur Beach - A popular beach destination with various water sports activities.
  • Tannirbhavi Beach - Known for its tranquil atmosphere and scenic beauty.

Relax and unwind amidst the soothing waves of Mangalore's beaches!

Nature and Parks:

  • Pilikula Nisargadhama - A nature park offering a range of recreational activities.
  • Sultan Battery - A historic watchtower with scenic views of the surrounding area.

Explore the natural beauty and historical landmarks of Mangalore!
